# Allamaprabhu S Ani

PhD candidate in computational mechanics and deep learning at **City, St George's, University of London**. Working on phase-field fracture, differentiable solvers, and physics-informed learning. Thesis target submission **July 2026**.

Founder of **[SynaCAD](https://github.com/allamaprabhuani/synacad)** — an AI design partner for mechanical engineering, bound by the rules of mechanics rather than by what an LLM thinks sounds plausible.
